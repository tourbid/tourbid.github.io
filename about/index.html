tourbid.in brings in a concept of bidding for your business & leisure travel requirements by connecting travellers with tour operators, cabbies, hotels alike. You just have to place your request for travel, say, 1 day trip from Bangalore to Mysore & back for 6 people on 10th October 2015 and wait for responses. The backend system automatically notifies registered cab operators/owners who can revert back with their quotes. Some key features include: - You will be be able to select your preferred cabbie based on the in-built recommendation system. - Post your trip, you are required to provide your feedback on the service which will aid future users. Similarly, operators too can leave a feedback on the user. - Reward system based on usage patterns as mutually agreed with users & providers.
